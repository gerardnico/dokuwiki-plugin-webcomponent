====== Combostrap - Unit ======


===== About =====
''unit'' is just a [[semantic|semantic component]] to indicate a unit of sequence of [[doku>wiki:syntax#code_blocks|code block]].

It doesn't render.

===== Articles Related =====
{{backlinks>.}}

===== Syntax =====

  * The unit node may got the ''display="none"'' property to not display the whole block
<code >
<unit display="none">
<file txt nico.txt>
Nico
</file>
%%A block code%%
<console>
Nico
</console>
</unit>
</code>
