====== WebComponent - Unit ======


===== About =====
''unit'' is just a semantic [[webcomponent|component]] to indicate a unit of sequence of [[doku>wiki:syntax#code_blocks|code block]].

It doesn't render.

===== Articles Related =====
{{backlinks>.}}

===== Syntax =====

<webcode name="Default" frameborder=0 width=100% scrolling=yes externalResources="," renderingMode=story >
<code dw>
<unit>
<file txt nico.txt>
Nico
</file>
%%A code block may also come here%%
<console>
Nico
</console>
</unit>
</code>
</webcode>
